IMPORTANT INFORMATION FOR THE EVENT

Hello schools!
Expo is this weekend and we are super excited to have your students! Below is a list of a few important reminders for this weekend:

  • Registration (Arriving on Buses) : When you arrive on the bus, we will have a team member stepping onto the bus and handing your bus drivers directions to Busch Gardens. Once our team member gets off the bus, please have one teacher get off the bus and give our team member the "School Name" and "Number of Students" while your students are unloading. Please unload the bus as quickly and safely as possible. We will be having over a hundred buses coming in and leaving.
  • Registration (Arriving in Vans/Parking in Beard): Please park on levels 4 - 8. There will be a registration table outside Beard Garage after you park. Please check in before heading to Expo.
  • APP: Please download Engineering Expo - University of South Florida app. This app will have the campus map along with all of the exhibits for Expo.
  • Exhibit Descriptions: Below we have attached a description of all the exhibits at Expo!
  • Maps: We have attached the map for Expo below. Your school will only be receiving one map when you arrive so please bring your own maps.
  • Self-guided Tour: Below is a self guided tour of our campus. Please print them out if your students would like to participate. We will not be handing these out at Expo.
  • Scavenger Hunt: Below is a scavenger hunt. You will receive only one copy the day of Expo, so please print them out if your students want to participate.
  • NO COOLERS: We are not responsible for watching your coolers throughout the day. If your school plans to leave the coolers on the bus, your students will not be able to go back to the buses because they will be parked off campus.
  • Teacher packet is attached below with more information on lunches, directors for the pick-up/drop-off location and more.
  • UPON ARRIVAL/DEPARTURE:
    • Bus directions: Your bus drivers will receive the attached document with directions for going to and from Busch Gardens where they will be parking. If your bus arrives before you arrive, they will be sent on a 15 minute loop. Please make sure you are in contact with your bus driver so you all are waiting to be picked up before the bus arrives.
    • Teacher directions: Please make sure you have a way to contact your bus driver when you are getting picked up. We suggest getting a cell phone number in order to make the pick up process simpler. When you arrive at the pick-up/drop off location, please have one teacher check in at our registration table.

If you have any questions please let us know! We look forward to seeing you this weekend!

PARKING FOR PARENTS AND GUESTS

If you are driving yourselves, free parking is available for parents and other guests in the Beard Parking garage for free on levels 4 to 8. There will be signs directing parents and other guests on where to park and exit to the event. Address: Richard A. Beard Parking Facility, Tampa, FL 33620
