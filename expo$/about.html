ABOUT USF ENGINEERING EXPO


Engineering Expo is a two-day open house event held annually at the University of South Florida's College of Engineering. EXPO is run entirely by students and has historically brought over 10,000 visitors to campus yearly.

EXPO showcases the College of Engineering, USF academic departments, research labs from across campus, student organizations, and exhibits from local engineering firms. Engineering Expo hosted more than 10,000 students, youth groups, and families in 2015. Large demonstration tables and shows presented by local engineering firms have included: TECO, NASA, Honeywell, Raytheon, Sypris Electronics, Lockheed Martin, and more!

Our Mission -
Engineering Expo is dedicated to introducing, inspiring, and enriching the community in STEM related studies. We provide a safe and entertaining environment for K-12 students and the populace to gain hands on experience, by connecting them with USF Student Organizations and industry professionals. This event allows USF student organizations to exhibit innovative student research, build rapport with faculty, community professionals, and engage in community outreach by continuing the legacy of the longest standing event at USF.


MEET THE BOARD


Director of Schools

Emily Pretorius and Megan Hostetter

The USF Engineering Expo School Board invites over 10,000 students of all ages, from schools all over Florida! Our board has the pleasure to communicate directly with educators and deliver the information needed to plan, arrive, and experience Expo smoothly. We orchestrate a unique school’s activity that inspires and cultivates a strong interest in STEM for the students. We also encourage a scavenger hunt that allows the students to roam USF’s Engineering area exposing them to our clubs, organizations, and exhibitors. We strive to show our visitors the real life application of how interdisciplinary and diverse engineering is.

Director of Exhibitors

Zachary Armstrong, Rachel Reinhart and Clarissa Arriaga

Our goal is to ensure that we can provide all the support necessary for exhibitors to create a lasting impression on our young visitors. We network with USF student organizations as well as STEM related organizations outside of USF and encourage them to participate in this event. Moreover, we also ensure that each project and/or exhibit follows the standard set as required by the Expo Board.

Director of Girl Scouts

Vanessa Forero and Patricia Tate

Girl Scouts aims to encourage and educate young girls about the field of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) with a focus on engineering. We strive to have Girl Scouts of all ages experience the processes of design, development, trial, and error through the activities we develop. These activities are also geared towards earning the Inventor Badge or the Product Designer Badge along with a fun patch designed by Engineering Expo.

Director of Logistics

Kateryna Turchenko, Kseniia Kalenina and Sofia Dao

The Logistics Board ensures that all required spaces, resources and utilities necessary to host the event are reserved. We also identify placement requirements for organizations and create the layout of the event for more than 50 exhibitors.

Director of Corporate

Victoria Poitier and Victoria Carlos

Corporate Directors network with various companies and encourage them to exhibit their latest innovations at the Engineering Expo. We also cooperate with the Director of Exhibitors to ensure that all the exhibits comply with Expo Board regulations. Apart from this, we also establish good relations with these companies to ensure that they will participate in the years to come.

Director of Volunteers

Katty Pierre-Charles and Luiza Oliveira

As Director of Volunteers, we are in-charge of making sure that there are a lot of helping hands the day of for set-up, during and after the event. Our goal is to train and make sure that these volunteers can help make the Expo a definite success.

Director of Marketing and Web Design

Sophia Hymes and Blanche Pinto

The USF Engineering Expo's Marketing and Web Design Board is responsible for creating and enhancing the organizations' presence amongst students, companies, and the community. We plan and design all the graphics ranging from logos to flyers and also this website! This board is crucial for the advertisement and promotion of the event!

Director of Community Outreach

Victoria Leppold and Austin Albrecht

Community Outreach strives to involve and excite the Tampa Bay residents and visitors by incentivizing their time to enjoy the annual USF Engineering Expo! This year, Community Outreach presents the STEM Lecture Series! Keynote speakers Dr. Ralph Fehr and Professor Antonio Paris will be exploring topics that are vital to the future of Earth and its inhabitants! Engineering in the Future will be presented both days of Engineering Expo at 11am and 1pm. Engineering on Mars will be presented only on Friday, February 17th at 12pm and 2pm.

Be sure to complete our activity booklet as you explore Expo. The first 30 people to finish and return to the Welcome Booth with a finished booklet will receive a secret prize! The next 1000 people will receive an official USF Engineering Expo Wristband.
