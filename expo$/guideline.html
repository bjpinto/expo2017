GUIDELINES FOR ATTENDEES

  1. 1. If you are a school group, make sure to check-in at registration tent next to Juniper-Poplar where the buses unload.
  2. 2. Check-in for anyone not affiliated with a school group will be at the Information tent next to building Engineering Building II (ENB).
  3. 3. Do not climb on walls, trees, or statues.
  4. 4. Trash is to thrown in garbage cans, not on the ground!
  5. 5. Be courteous in labs and classrooms (do not pull eyewash stations, yell indoors, run indoors, etc.)
  6. 6. USF is not responsible for lost or stolen belongings, keep track of your things! We will have a Lost and Found box at the Information Tent, but that is all we will do.
  7. 7. Wear comfortable clothing and shoes, there will be a lot of walking and a lot of time spent outside; there may be long lines. Dress for the weather.
  8. 8. Maps and Parking information will be put on the website as Expo draws closer.
